Refunds:

1) We kindly ask that you get in touch with us within 24 hours of delivery if the product arrives to you in a state that makes it inappropriate for use, has passed its expiration date, etc. If necessary, we will investigate the problem through photographs or in person at our warehouse. If we have determined that the product has reached you in the manner described above, we will start the refund process within 48 hours.

2) Since everyone may not have the same or similar taste preferences, we do not accept "Taste" as a reason for returning the product.

3) We only accept returns for packaged/branded food items if they have passed their expiration dates or for similar reasons; if there are any quality-related issues.

How refunds processed?

Within two days of the issue being acknowledged, the refund process will begin. Except for COD, it will be processed to the original payment method. If you choose COD, our team will get in touch with you to get the information for your bank account where the money can be credited.
