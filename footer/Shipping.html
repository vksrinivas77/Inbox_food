Shipping Policy

1. Free Shipping on Orders Above Rs. 400: We provide free shipping on all orders above Rs. 400 across India.

2. Shipping Charges on Orders Below Rs. 399: Orders below Rs. 399 will be charged a nominal shipping fee of Rs. 50.

3. Cash On Delivery Charges: We charge a Cash on Delivery (COD) fee of Rs. 30 on all COD orders.

4. Handover Time: We will hand over your order to our delivery partners within 1 day from the date of order confirmation.

5. Delivery Time: We deliver orders within the following timelines:

  • Metro Cities: 2-3 working days
  • Rest of India: 6-7 working days
  • Semi-Metro Cities: 4-6 working days

6. Delivery Partners: We work with trusted delivery partners to ensure timely and safe delivery of your orders.

7. Order Tracking: We provide tracking information for your order once it is shipped, allowing you to track the status of your order.

8. Delivery Address: We deliver to all locations across India that are serviceable by our delivery partners. However, there may be certain locations that are not serviceable. In such cases, we will cancel the order and inform you of the same.

9. Delayed or Failed Delivery: In case of a delay or failed delivery, we will notify you of the same and make every effort to resolve the issue at the earliest.

10. Order Cancellation: Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped. Once the order is shipped, it cannot be cancelled.

11. Return Policy: We have a hassle-free return policy in case you receive a damaged or incorrect product. Please refer to our return policy page for more details.

We hope this shipping policy meets your requirements. If you have any further questions or clarifications, please do not hesitate to contact us.
